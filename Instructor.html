About Me

As an Automotive Technology Instructor at Central Wyoming College (CWC), I am dedicated to preparing students for the rapidly evolving automotive industry. Joining the faculty in 2023, I focus on equipping students with the technical skills necessary to service the next generation of vehicles, including hybrid and electric technologies.

My goal is to provide hands-on training that bridges the gap between traditional mechanical knowledge and modern automotive diagnostics.
